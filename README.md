### So what is Resveratrol?
Resveratrol is a stilbenoid, a type of natural phenol and a phytoalexin produced by several plants when the plant is under attack by pathogens such as bacteria or fungi. Resveratrol is widespread in our daily food such as grapes, peanuts and so on.
